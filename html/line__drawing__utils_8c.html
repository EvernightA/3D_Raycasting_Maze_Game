Paint a Strip of Wall with Pretty Wallpaper! ðŸŽ¨

Imagine you're decorating a long wall, but you do it one tiny section at a time. This function paints just one vertical sliver of wall (like a candy cane stripe) at a specific spot on your screen.

It figures out:

  • Which wallpaper to use for this wall
  • How tall the stripe should be (based on how far away you are)
  • Where on the screen to paint it
  • Which part of the wallpaper to copy for each pixel

It's like having a tiny painter robot that carefully copies wallpaper patterns onto your 3D walls, one stripe at a time!

Parameters
displayYour whole 3D world with walls and wallpapers
xWhich column on the screen to paint (like 100th pixel from the left)
rayYour laser pointer showing exactly where and how far the wall is
